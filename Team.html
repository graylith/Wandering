Meet our Team

Shashikant Singh

Shashikant Singh

Founder /Managing Director

Wandering is what made us explorers, travelling aimlessly lead us to destinations which humans cherish and feel delighted to share with the present as well as the future generation.

As a wanderer, I have travelled different states of India, visiting and observing the different lifestyles which our country has to offer. This helped me gain an insight into what it means to travel and who is the traveller. Born and bought up in Bihar, during my school days, I participated in various sports events and extracurricular activities which gave me my first experience of travel. I had the opportunity of getting my higher education from Delhi University. Being the joint secretary in the student union enshrined leadership qualities in me and NCC empowered me with the skills to survive in a tougher situation. After studying ‘Travel and Tourism’ in my academics, I realised the potential that this industry has for me as well as for society.

Connecting people from different cultures, in a place which they are new to, gives an entirely different sense of contentment and happiness. A wholesome experience of travel helps to bridge the gap with one’s self and with the rest of the world.


Anuj Pawar

Anuj Pawar

Co-founder / Team Leader

This human life is our best and only chance to live and experience this planet. Limiting this fantastic experience to a single place would be a significant loss!.

The only way to truly live the human experience is by getting out of your comfort zone and exploring new places and cultures. Travelling is a process of learning; a process of getting to know oneself.

I did my college from Delhi University, and that’s where I became aware of the diversity of my nation. India’s travel sector has excellent potential for growth. We should make the world aware of our country’s capacity to offer novel and rich experiences. I did my diploma in travel tourism from YMCA NEW DELHI. I am also a pistol shooter at U.P. state rifle association.

I feel that in the race of survival, somewhere we have forgotten to explore our incredible planet.

Go ahead and see it for yourself, allow yourself to step out into the unknown and transform into a new person. I promise it’ll change the way you see things and lead you to that inner peace we all crave.


Abhishek Rawat

Abhishek Rawat

Operational Head / Fitness Instructor

‘Travel’ is an essential aspect of human life and evolution.

This is Abhishekrawat, I’m a movement artist, and hip-hop head represents universal Hip-hop culture chapter Delhi, India.

Since 2009 and I practice every possible movement human body could make or even go beyond limitation, I’ve been exploring movement art over a decade in various dance styles/movement of different culture like as breakin (hip-hop), House dance, afro styles, popping, locking, Martial arts, Hatha yoga and capoeira with artist from all around the globe and ‘travel’ plays a vital role in this journey of learning & experience.

My journey has been started since my childhood, I used to visit my hometown ‘Uttrakhand’ in summer/winter vacation every year and then when I got into movement art Boom, I never stopped, and this is inspired to take the initiative to spread the knowledge to all life on this planet.
